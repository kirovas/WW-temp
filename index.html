About us

Crew Recruitment Services is a manning agency which provides high quality seafarers of all ranks on the foreign going vessels and offshore units. We provide seafarers for work at offshore installations and such offshore units as oil derricks, wind generators, etc. Founded in 2007, our manning agency has become a leader in its segment. Our principle is not only the fast job connection. We sample our seafarers according their experience, skills and psychological correspondence in order to create strong and high quality crew. We guarantee active teamwork with a client and attention to details. Work at offshore units and merchant vessels require responsible and strong crew which can be provided by Crew Recruitment Services.
